EXECUTIVE SUMMARY\n\nThis report synthesizes findings from multiple studies on the healing process of severe left-hand distal radius fractures treated with plate insertion, focusing on healing time, functional outcomes, and complications. INTRODUCTION AND CONTEXT\n\nDistal radius fractures are among the most common fractures, significantly impacting hand function and quality of life.  This research investigates the healing process of severe left-hand distal radius fractures treated with plate insertion, aiming to identify factors influencing healing time, functional outcomes, and complication rates.  A systematic approach was used to analyze data from various studies, including randomized controlled trials (RCTs), retrospective studies, and meta-analyses, covering diverse aspects of treatment and rehabilitation. \nVolar locked plate (VLP) fixation shows superior outcomes in several studies.
